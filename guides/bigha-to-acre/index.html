Bigha to Acre Conversion Formula

The conversion formula for bigha to acre depends on which state you're measuring in. However, the basic principle is simple: multiply your bigha value by the conversion factor for your state.

Bigha × State Conversion Factor = Acre

For example, if you have 5 bigha in Uttar Pradesh and want to know how many acres that is:

5 bigha × 0.4 = 2 acres (in Uttar Pradesh)

This formula works because bigha and acre are both measures of land area, just in different units. Bigha is a traditional Indian measurement, while acre is an international standard used across India, Britain, and the United States.
