Kanal to Acre Conversion Formula

The kanal to acre conversion is the simplest land measurement conversion in India, with a perfect ratio:

Kanal × 0.25 = Acre

For example, if you have 8 kanals and want to know how many acres that is:

8 kanals × 0.25 = 2 acres

This simple 4:1 ratio makes kanal conversions easy for mental math and quick calculations. The kanal measurement is primarily used in Punjab and Haryana, making it the standard unit for property transactions in North-Western India.

Kanal is the primary land measurement unit in Punjab and Haryana. It's used extensively in property transactions, government land records, and agricultural measurements in these two states. While bigha is used in other North Indian states, kanal is the standard in Punjab and Haryana.
