Bigha to Hectare Conversion Formula

The conversion from bigha to hectare requires dividing the bigha size by 10,000 (since a hectare equals 10,000 square meters). The exact conversion depends on your state's bigha size.

Bigha (in Sq M) ÷ 10,000 = Hectare

For example, if you have 5 bigha in Uttar Pradesh and want to know how many hectares that is:

5 bigha × 1,618.74 sq m = 8,093.7 sq m ÷ 10,000 = 0.81 hectares

Hectare is the international standard metric measurement for land area. One hectare equals 2.471 acres or 10,000 square meters. This makes hectare to bigha conversion essential for government records, scientific measurements, and international property documentation.
